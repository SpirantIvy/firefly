Visual Comp

This website was designed with a mail dark color to give the feeling of night, with pops of a soft orange to give a comforting feel to the viewer to reflect the warmth of our platform. The orange symbolizes energy, and the target audience is young people who want to connect and have fun with their friends like they used to.



The Idea:
The feeling given should make you feel want for connection and a desire to make new memories with your friends and the people you like or want to connect with. 



How I did it:
This site uses typography, layout, alignment, visual hierarchy, and color to make it stand out and give a feeling of connection and togetherness. 



Where it's used:
Typography, layouts, alignment and visual hierarchy are used in the text, headers, and images to make everything easier to read and to help give the simple idea of togetherness in the simplest way.

Color is used to make the text and icons pop, while also giving this site a relevant theme to what we do. Virtual campfires.
